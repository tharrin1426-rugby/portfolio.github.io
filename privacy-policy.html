Last updated: July 2, 2026

This policy explains what data this site collects, why, and the rights you have over it under the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable US state privacy laws (including the California Consumer Privacy Act / CPRA, Colorado Privacy Act, Connecticut Data Privacy Act, and Virginia Consumer Data Protection Act).

Who is responsible for your data

This site is operated by Thomas J. Harrington. For any privacy question or request, contact invictus.marketing.td@gmail.com.

What data is collected

Analytics (only if you consent): When you accept analytics cookies via the consent banner, Google Analytics (via Google Tag Manager) collects approximate location (derived from IP), device/browser type, pages viewed, referring site, and on-site interactions (e.g. link clicks, resume downloads, section views). No analytics data is collected before you consent.

Contact form: If you use the contact form, the name, email address, subject, and message you enter are opened in your own email client via a mailto: link. This data is never transmitted to or stored on any server operated by this site — it is sent directly from your device using your own email provider.

Analytics cookies are set only on the basis of your explicit consent (Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR). You may withdraw consent at any time via the "Cookie Preferences" link in the site footer, with no effect on the lawfulness of processing before withdrawal.

Third parties

Analytics data (if you consent) is processed by Google LLC via Google Analytics and Google Tag Manager, which may transfer data outside the EEA under Google's Standard Contractual Clauses. This site does not sell or share personal information with third parties for cross-context behavioral advertising.

Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the right to access, rectify, erase, restrict, or port your data, to object to processing, to withdraw consent at any time, and to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

Under US state laws (CCPA/CPRA and similar), you have the right to know what personal information is collected, to request deletion or correction, and to opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information — this site honors the Global Privacy Control (GPC) browser signal automatically as an opt-out request.

Data retention

Your consent choice is stored in your browser's local storage until you change it or clear your browser data. Analytics data retention is governed by Google Analytics' default retention settings.

Children's privacy

This site is not directed at children and does not knowingly collect data from anyone under 18.
